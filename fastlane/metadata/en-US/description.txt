Usbong is a social business whose mission is to defeat poverty by uplifting human lives through our thrusts in education and healthcare. Our vision is to build a community where there is lasting peace.
Therefore, we provide training and workshops on Android development, Software Engineering, and Social Entrepreneurship. We also offer software development services.

Our flagship product is also called Usbong. Together with its companion Android app, Usbong Builder, it is an open source platform that helps even non-technical people build their own Android apps for use in data collection, assessment, training, screening, diagnostics, and also multilingual interactive storytelling.
